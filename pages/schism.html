The Schism

The galaxy was a peaceful place, until Zorghanski came to power. The evil dictator turned the galaxy into a New Monarch. Everyone except for those loyal to Zorghanski revolted against him, forming factions which fight to rule over the galaxy. Now, there is an interstellar war based off ideological differences.
