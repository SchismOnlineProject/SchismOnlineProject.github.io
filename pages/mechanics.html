Mechanics of Schism Online

There are many planned mechanics for Schism Online, here are just a few.

Psuedo-Permadeath If a character dies, everything they were carrying and an amount of their wealth is forfiet to who/what killed them. The player's skills are halved.
Survival Players will need to keep their characters fed, hydrated, warm, and rested in order to be in peak condition.
PvP There will be mutliple dedicated PvP worlds in between faction controlled star systems and one huge PvP world at the center of the faction controlled star systems.
PvE The worlds within the faction controlled star systems are all PvE worlds, where players don't have to worry about enemy attacks.
